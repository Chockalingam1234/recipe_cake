Chocolate Cake

"This chocolate cake is a classic. This version of the cake is fast and quick and your kids will love it".

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10min 25min 35min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate : 200g

Sugar : 150g

Flour : 80g

Eggs : 4

Instructions

1

Melt the Chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and Flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
